      FAQ
    
    
    
      
      
        
          
            
              
                Is the music I create with SOUNDRAW really royalty free and safe for monetization?
              
              
                

              
            
            
                

                  Yes. SOUNDRAW's AI is trained only on songs our in-house producers write and record, so every beat you generate comes with a worldwide, perpetual license. Post your track on YouTube, Spotify, TikTok, or anywhere else and keep 100% of the revenue—no copyright claims, ever.
                
            
          
          
            
              
                Can I download individual STEMS and edit them in my DAW?
              
              
                

              
            
            
                

                  Absolutely. When downloading hit "WAV + Stems" and you'll get separate WAV files for drums, bass, melody, vocals (if any), and FX. Drop them into Ableton, Logic, FL Studio, or any DAW to tweak mix levels, add plugins, or record vocals on top.
                
            
          
          
            
              
                What makes SOUNDRAW different from other AI music generators?
              
              
                

              
            
            
                

                  Most AI tools scrape existing songs off the internet. SOUNDRAW's catalog is 100% self-produced, which means no legal grey areas. You also get bar-level editing (mute, solo, intensity), genre-blending, and STEM exports—features most generators still lack.
                
            
          
          
            
              
                Do I need any music experience to use SOUNDRAW?
              
              
                

              
            
            
                

                  Nope. Pick a genre (Hip-Hop, EDM, Lo-Fi, 30+ more), set the mood, and hit Generate. The AI builds the arrangement; you just use SOUNDRAW's in-app editor to refine length, energy, or instruments. No theory, no DAW, no problem.
                
            
          
          
            
              
                How does the subscription pricing work—are downloads really unlimited?
              
              
                

              
            
            
                
                  

Creators Plan (one tier): pay monthly or annually and you can generate and download as many tracks (.mp3 only) as you want—there's literally no cap. Anything you make while you're subscribed stays licensed for life, even if you cancel later.

Artists Plan (three tiers):

  - Artist Starter – lets you download up to 10 fully-mixed songs (.mp3 only) per month—perfect if you just need a handful of beats to write over.

  - Artist Pro – bumps the monthly allowance to 20 songs and includes .wav, .mp3 and STEMS, giving you enough inventory for regular single drops or EPs.

  - Artist Unlimited – removes the cap entirely, matching the Creators plan's "all-you-can-eat" model while also providing .wav and STEM downloads

All Artist tiers include a perpetual commercial license, and 100% royalty ownership on streaming platforms. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time, and anything you've already exported remains covered forever.
