## 

Play as Hololive's ace detective Amelia WATson as she investigates the kingdom of Chicago in search of mythical creatures.

This game is a short turn-based RPG based on  [Ame's first AI Dungeon stream](https://youtu.be/Fwx9HyfJoSw), and all the shenanigans that happen in it.

"You are Detective Watson, a detective living in Chicago. You have a pistol and a police badge. You enter the forest where you believe the criminal you're searching for fled to."


The game was made by  [fie](https://twitter.com/zkfie). If something breaks, throw a rock at him. 


Controls



**WASD / Arrows**         - move

**Shift   **                                - run

**Z / ****,      **                               - interact

**X / .       **                              - cancel

**ESC       **                             - open the game menu
