After a first hotfix to the recent 4.6 release, we now have a second maintenance release in the form of Godot 4.6.2, adding a number of important bug fixes and improvements to the stable branch.

Maintenance releases are expected to be safe for an upgrade, but we recommend to always make backups, or use a version control system such as Git, to preserve your projects in case of corruption or data loss.

Please consider supporting the project financially[godotengine.org], if you are able. Godot is maintained by the efforts of volunteers and a small team of paid contributors. Your donations go towards sponsoring their work and ensuring they can dedicate their undivided attention to the needs of the project.
